Program Overview

Workshops and tutorials will take place on Saturday and Sunday (July 12th & 13th, 2014). Talks and poster sessions will take place from Monday to Wednesday (July 14th & 16th, 2014). A detailed technical program is also available.

Breakfast 8:30-9:00 on Sat, Mon, Tue, Wed. Breakfast 8:00-8:30 on Sun.

Date8:30am-6pm6pm onward
SatWorkshops & Tutorials
SunWorkshops & Tutorials
MonConferenceWelcome Reception 1
TueConferenceConference Banquet 2
WedConferenceDinner & Demos at Google HQ 3

1 Welcome Reception (sponsored by Anki): We will celebrate the 10th edition of RSS with cake and drinks. Anki will have demos of the Anki Drive game for RSS attendees to try out.

2 Conference Banquet: The conference banquet will be held on a 2-hour cruise in the San Francisco Bay. Enjoy a splendid sunset on the water with magnificent views of the city of San Francisco, accompanied by a delicious seasonal dinner and drinks. Transportation will be provided.

3 Dinner & Demos at Google HQ: On the last day of the conference, Google will be hosting a special evening event for RSS attendees at the Google Headquarters in Mountain View. Register for the event (by marking the appropriate option while registering for RSS 2014) and you can look forward to a night of hosted dinner and demos from some of the Google teams working on robotics, machine learning, and computer vision. Teams that will be present include the Self-Driving Car team, Project Loon, Google Glass, Streetview, and more. Transportation will be provided. The end-time at Google is 9:30pm, estimated time to be back in Berkeley 10:40pm.

Update: The Dinner & Demos event at Google HQ has exceeded maximum capacity and registration is now closed for this event.
