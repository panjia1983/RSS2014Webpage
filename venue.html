Parking

Parking places nearest to Wheeler Hall:


View parking nearest to Wheeler Hall in a larger map

Lower Hearst Parking Structure: Full-day parking fee: $15. The parking meter only accepts coins/bills.

RSF Parking Lot: Full-day parking fee: $15. The parking meter only accepts coins/bills.

Bancroft Parking Structure: Full-day parking fee: $15. The parking meter only accepts coins/bills.

Telegraph-Channing Garage (formally Sather Gate Garage): Full-day parking fee: $15. The parking meter accepts coins/bills/credit cards.

Please make sure that you park in designated areas only. City of Berkeley and UC Berkeley enforce parking rules super-strictly and will ticket violators.
