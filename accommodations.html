Accommodation Information

The following accommodation options are available.

1. A limited number of rooms at the student dorms on the Berkeley campus are reserved for RSS 2014 from July 11, 2014 through July 16, 2014. The dorms are within a 10 minute walk of the conference site. The dorm address is:

Unit 2 Residence Hall
2650 Haste Street
Berkeley, CA 94720

These rooms will be allocated on a first come, first-serve basis. Preference will be given to students though all attendees are eligible to stay in the dorms. Two kinds of rooms are available:
a. Double occupancy room ($355.04 for conference and workshops duration)
b. Single occupancy room ($511.04 for conference and workshops duration)
Check-in starts on Friday 7/11 after 3:00 pm, check-out is before noon on Thursday 7/17. Breakfast is included in the rate.

Information about dorm reservations is forthcoming.

For individuals arriving before or staying after RSS conference date may make reservations through Summer Visitor Housing (see this link for additional information).

2. A room block is being held at the Hotel Shattuck Plaza, a full-service hotel within walking distance of the conference venue (10 minute walk from the conference site). The hotel is at:

Hotel Shattuck Plaza
2086 Allston Way
Berkeley, CA 94704

To reserve rooms at the Hotel Shattuck Plaza at the RSS 2014 rate, please visit this reservation link and use the promotion code 1407ROBOTI.

The RSS rate for July 11, 2014 through July 16, 2014 is
a. Deluxe King Bedroom ($165 per night)
b. Deluxe Two Double Beds ($175 per night)

Another option is to call the hotel directly 510-845-7300 or toll free 866-466-9199 and ask for the Robotic Science and Systems Conference. Please work with Bernadette Stafford at the Hotel Shattuck Plaza if you need help obtaining the RSS rate outside the conference dates. This room block at the special rate is available through May 11, 2014 or when all the rooms in the block are reserved (whichever is earlier).
