2014 Robotics: Science and Systems Conference

The 2014 Robotics: Science and Systems Conference will bring together researchers working on algorithmic or mathematical foundations of robotics, robotics applications, and analysis of robotic systems. High quality, original papers are solicited in all areas of robotics. The final program will be the result of a highly selective review process designed to include the best work of its kind in every category. The conference will be single track to allow attendees an opportunity to experience the best research in all areas of robotics. The program will include invited talks as well as oral and poster presentations of refereed papers.

The website of RSS 2013 in Berlin can be found here


Conference Location

The conference will be held on Campus at UC Berkeley, in Berkeley, California, USA.
