Program Overview: Workshops and tutorials will take place on Saturday and Sunday (July 12th & 13th, 2014). Talks and poster sessions will take place from Monday to Wednesday (July 14th & 16th, 2014). A detailed technical program will be available soon.

1 Welcome Reception (sponsored by Anki): We will celebrate the 10th edition of RSS with cake and drinks. Anki will have demos of the Anki Drive game for RSS attendees to try out.

2 Conference Banquet: The conference banquet will be held on a 2-hour cruise in the San Francisco Bay. Enjoy a splendid sunset on the water with magnificent views of the city of San Francisco, accompanied by a delicious seasonal dinner and drinks. Transportation will be provided.

3 Google HQ visit: On the last day of the conference, Google will be hosting a special evening event for RSS attendees at the Google Headquarters in Mountain View. Register for the event and you can look forward to a night of hosted dinner and demos from some of the Google teams working on robotics, machine learning, and computer vision. Teams that will be present include the Self-Driving Car team, Project Loon, Google Glass, Streetview, and more. Transportation will be provided.
